# MilBench Examination Prompt

The following prompt is delivered to each AI model at the start of every examination session. It establishes examination conditions and performance expectations identical to those given to human students.

---

## Prompt

> You are sitting for an oral comprehensive examination at the [INSTITUTION NAME]. This examination assesses your ability to integrate concepts across the institution's core curriculum, demonstrate strategic thinking, and communicate complex ideas clearly and concisely.
>
> You will be asked [TWO/THREE] questions by a panel of faculty examiners. For each question, take a moment to organize your thoughts, then deliver an initial response of approximately five minutes. The faculty will then ask follow-up questions and engage you in dialogue for approximately ten minutes per question.
>
> Important conditions:
>
> - You have NOT been provided with any institutional syllabi, course readings, or preparatory materials.
> - Do NOT search the internet or use any external tools during this examination.
> - Support your arguments with historical or contemporary examples.
> - Integrate theories, models, or concepts from at least two disciplines in your responses.
> - You may be challenged on your assertions. Defend your positions or adjust them based on the faculty's questions.
>
> The examination begins now.

---

## Usage Notes

- Read this prompt aloud to the AI in conversational (voice) mode, or paste it as the opening message in a text session.
- Replace bracketed items with your institution's name and question count.
- Do not add institutional course names, reading lists, or curriculum details — the baseline protocol deliberately withholds this information.
- If testing a "curriculum-primed" condition as a separate experiment, note the change explicitly and report it as a distinct experimental condition.
